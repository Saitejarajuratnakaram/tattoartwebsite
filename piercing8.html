Nose Piercing

Nose Piercing is a popular body modification that involves creating a hole in the nostril or septum to insert jewelry. This piercing has a long history across various cultures and is known for its aesthetic appeal and cultural significance.

This piercing can be adorned with various jewelry styles, such as studs, hoops, and septum rings. Nose piercings are celebrated for their versatility and can be styled to complement both casual and formal looks.

What We Do

Various Placement Options

Nose piercings can be placed in different areas, such as the nostril or septum, offering a range of options to suit personal style and preferences.

Diverse Jewelry Options

Nose piercings can be adorned with various jewelry styles, including studs, hoops, and septum rings. This versatility allows for a wide range of personal expression and customization.

Moderate Healing Time

While nose piercings generally heal well, the healing time can vary depending on the placement and aftercare. Typically, it ranges from 4 to 6 weeks for nostril piercings and 6 to 8 weeks for septum piercings.
