Customer Questions & Answers

Q1: What should I do before getting a piercing?

A: Ensure you are well-hydrated, avoid alcohol for 24 hours before your appointment, and get a good night’s sleep. Our piercers will also provide specific pre-care instructions.

Q2: How do I care for my new piercing?

A: Follow the aftercare instructions provided, which typically include keeping the piercing clean, applying antiseptic, and avoiding unnecessary touching and irritation.

Q3: Can I bring my own jewelry?

A: We recommend using our high-quality jewelry for your initial piercing to ensure proper fit and safety. However, we can discuss bringing your own jewelry upon consultation.

Q4: What is the minimum age requirement?

A: The minimum age for getting a piercing is 18, even with parental consent.
