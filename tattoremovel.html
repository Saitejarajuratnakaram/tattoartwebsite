Customer Questions & Answers

Q1: What should I do before getting a tattoo removal?

A: Avoid sun exposure, refrain from using any harsh skincare products on the area, and discuss any medications or skin conditions with our technician during your consultation.

Q2: How do I care for my skin after a tattoo removal session?

A: Follow the aftercare instructions provided, which typically include keeping the area clean, applying healing ointment, and avoiding direct sunlight and strenuous activities.

Q3: Can I get another tattoo after removal?

A: Yes, you can get another tattoo after the skin has fully healed from the removal process. It's best to wait a few months to ensure your skin is completely healed.

Q4: Are there any risks associated with tattoo removal?

A: While tattoo removal is generally safe, there can be risks such as scarring, skin discoloration, or infection if aftercare instructions are not followed. Our team will provide detailed aftercare guidelines to minimize risks.
